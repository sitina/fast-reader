# Privacy Policy for Fast Reader

**Last updated:** January 2026

## Overview

Fast Reader is a Chrome extension that helps users read web content faster using RSVP (Rapid Serial Visual Presentation). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Data Collection

**Fast Reader does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### What We Store Locally

The extension stores the following preferences on your device using Chrome's local storage API:

- Reading speed (words per minute)
- Smart pauses setting (on/off)

This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to any server
- Is not shared with any third party
- Can be cleared by removing the extension

### What We Access

When you use the extension, it temporarily accesses:

- **Page content**: To extract readable text from the current webpage. This text is processed entirely within your browser and is never stored or transmitted.
- **Selected text**: If you choose to read a text selection, that selected text is processed locally.

This content is:
- Only accessed when you explicitly click "Read This Page" or "Read Selection"
- Processed entirely in your browser
- Not stored after you close the reader
- Never transmitted to any external server

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `activeTab` | Access the current tab's content when you click the extension |
| `storage` | Save your reading speed preferences locally |
| `scripting` | Inject the reader interface into web pages |

## Third-Party Services

Fast Reader does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking.

## Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and no data is transmitted externally, there is no risk of data breach from our extension.

## Children's Privacy

Fast Reader does not collect any data from any users, including children.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.

## Contact
