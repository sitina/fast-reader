# Chrome Web Store Listing

## Extension Name
Fast Reader

## Short Description (132 characters max)
Speed read any webpage using RSVP. Words display one at a time with optimal recognition point highlighting for faster reading.

## Detailed Description

Fast Reader uses RSVP (Rapid Serial Visual Presentation) to help you read web content faster. Instead of moving your eyes across lines of text, words are displayed one at a time at a fixed position - eliminating eye movement and increasing reading speed.

KEY FEATURES

- Optimal Recognition Point (ORP): Each word is aligned on its optimal recognition point (approximately 1/3 from the start), highlighted in red for instant recognition
- Adjustable Speed: Choose your reading pace from 100 to 800 words per minute
- Smart Pauses: Automatic longer pauses after punctuation and for longer words
- Two Reading Modes: Read the full article or just your selected text
- Keyboard Controls: Space to play/pause, arrow keys to navigate, Esc to close
- Dark Theme: Comfortable reading experience

HOW TO USE

1. Navigate to any article or webpage
2. Click the Fast Reader icon
3. Choose "Read This Page" or select text and click "Read Selection"
4. Adjust speed as needed using the slider or arrow keys

BACKED BY RESEARCH

RSVP reading has been studied extensively and shows benefits for various readers:
- Improved reading speed without significant comprehension loss at moderate speeds
- Particularly helpful for readers with ADHD or visual processing differences
- Effective for people with central vision loss

PRIVACY FOCUSED

Fast Reader processes all text locally in your browser. No data is collected, stored, or transmitted. Your reading preferences are saved only on your device.

Open source and free forever.
